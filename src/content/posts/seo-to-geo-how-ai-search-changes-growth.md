## What Is GEO?

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization — is the practice of structuring content so that AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) can understand, cite, and recommend it.

The shift: from ranking on a results page to being cited in an AI-generated answer.

## What Changes

**Authority signals matter more.** LLMs prefer sources with clear expertise markers, structured data, and consistent topical depth.

**Structure matters differently.** Schema markup, FAQ patterns, and clear H1/H2 hierarchy help both traditional crawlers and LLM parsers extract meaning.

**Content depth beats keyword density.** AI search engines synthesize across multiple sources. Thin content optimized for keywords gets ignored in favor of comprehensive, well-structured pages.

## What Stays the Same

- Site speed and technical health
- Quality backlinks
- Clear topical authority
- Good internal linking
