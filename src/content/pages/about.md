## Short Intro

I am an AI Product Manager interested in agentic workflows, ontology-driven AI systems, and growth experiments. My work sits between product strategy, technical prototyping, enterprise AI adoption, and distribution.

I explore how product managers can use AI coding tools, ontology thinking, and SEO/GEO growth systems to turn ambiguous business problems into working prototypes and public playbooks.

## What I Build

- Ontology-driven AI systems that turn fragmented enterprise data into structured, queryable knowledge
- Agent workflows with tools, memory, and human-in-the-loop governance
- AI PM playbooks and frameworks for working in the AI-native era
- Prototypes using vibe coding — LLM pair programming with Claude Code and Cursor
- SEO/GEO growth systems for B2B content and AI search visibility

## What I Am Currently Exploring

- How ontology and knowledge graphs make enterprise AI more reliable and operational
- How PMs can use vibe coding to validate ideas faster and communicate better with engineering
- How AI search (GEO) changes the way products get discovered and adopted
- Building in public — sharing frameworks, experiments, and lessons

## Selected Background

My background combines AI product work, industrial operations, data-driven growth, and engineering-oriented education. I have worked across industrial AI, SEO growth, product investment analysis, and B2B product contexts. I hold a Master of Engineering from Cornell University and a Bachelor of Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
